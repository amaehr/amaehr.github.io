About

I am a programming user designer & researcher with an educational background in cognitive science and the humanities. Through my professional experience, I have learned how to use my academic training in research methods together with my data processing skills to operationalise data-driven insights into user behaviour and user experience. In my last job, I have been a project manager within the UXLab and developed new test methods within R&D. Currently, I work as a UX designer and researcher at Ambu where I shape HCP's experiences on endoscopy monitors. Improving products to make them easier to use and fun to interact with is what drives and motivates me.

OpenMuseum

The open-museum is an independent digital museum and was created as part of the #VersusVirus hackathon within 48 hours. It is important to us that despite the restrictions under the Covid 19 pandemic, art and culture can be discovered together. The audio chat function enables communication with other visitors - almost like in a “real” museum. Our museum is built entirely on public domain content. You can browse our collection or check out the source code and help us improve our code.
