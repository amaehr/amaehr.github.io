01

Archetypes

For internal stakeholders we have created user archetype descriptions for print and digital media. More fitting for a B2B context, the archetypes focused on our users mindset, motivations, needs and behaviours rather than demographics and personal life stories. Through foundational explorative research, followed by validating user interviews, 6 parameters with which the 4 archetypes can be described have been identified. Since their introduction, the archetypes have been added to our Atlassian products as personas and are used during the SAFe PI plannings.

03

Mental models

When communicating complex, abstract or invisible topics, humans often use mental models in figurative speech or visual communication. Together with colleagues and researchers within microbiology, we investigated how our mental models, represented in illustrations, affect HCPs decisions for treatment plans. This allowed us further, to create updated communication material, drawing on existing mental models while addressing overseen complexity in a congruent manner. Read article for more information.
